Hi! I study various forms of higher category theory, algebraic K-theory, and polynomial functors. I am also interested in combinatorial topology, applied category theory, and homotopy type theory.

I am currently a Whyburn Research Associate and Lecturer (postdoc) in mathematics at the University of Virginia. I was previously a researcher at Topos Institute, and I recently completed my PhD in mathematics from Cornell University, advised by Inna Zakharevich.
