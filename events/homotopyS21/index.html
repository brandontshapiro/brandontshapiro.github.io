Homotopy Group Student Seminar

This webpage is to maintain the schedule for our student seminar focused on topics in homotopy theory, broadly construed, for the Spring 2021 semester. The format is to alternate giving a series of 1-2 talks, with the first intended as an approachable introduction to the speaker's chosen topic. See here for the Fall 2020 seminar.
